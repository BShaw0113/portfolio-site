How I Work

I approach problems with clarity, structure, and outcomes—so fixes stick and teams move faster.

1) Diagnose

Confirm scope, reproduce the issue, review logs/metrics, and identify root cause.

2) Improve

Implement stable fixes and configuration updates with minimal disruption.

3) Document

Create clear notes, steps, and handoffs so teams can repeat the solution.

4) Measure

Track outcomes (repeat incidents, resolution speed, stability) and iterate.
